Cyber Threats 101

Learn to recognize common online threats so you can protect your data and your peace of mind.

Phishing 🐟

Fake emails or websites trying to steal your personal info.

  • Fake bank alerts
  • Delivery scams
  • Phony DMs
  • “Reset your password” traps

Ransomware 💸

Malware that locks your files until a payment is made.

  • Email attachment viruses
  • Fake software updates
  • System freeze attacks

Social Engineering 🎭

Tricks that manipulate you into giving away info.

  • Fake tech support
  • Emotional manipulation
  • “Urgent CEO request” emails
